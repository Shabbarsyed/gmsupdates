About

Global Marketing Services is a leading and distinguished medical company of Pakistan. Part of an international group of companies, viSole, our focus areas are; biomedical investigation, research, life sciences, blood banking, hematology, medical devices, industry segments and pharmaceuticals

We have achieved sustained ranking in the top quartile of our industry and have become the first medical company of Pakistan to earn certification for ISO 9001:2015 & Good Distribution Practice for Devices (GDPMD)

Serving health industry since 2000, we have steadily expanded our line of products and created a presence in various diagnostic markets. We have established repute for providing quality solutions, excellent customer services, and timely product delivery. With a broad portfolio of diagnostic equipment, our clients include researchers, physicians, patients, hospitals, laboratories, and blood banks around the world
